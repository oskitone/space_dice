title: Space Dice - electronic dice noise machine description: Combination space laser noise and electronic dice machine category: Gadgets -> Portable Devices / Toys & Games -> Dice tags: toy gaming dice noise electronics openscad opensource oskitone These are the 3D-print files for the Oskitone Space Dice, a combination space laser noise and electronic dice machine. Press its button to get a random number between one and six _and_ a near-faithful recreation of classic science fiction blaster firing sounds. Its accompanying DIY Electronics Kit requires through-hole soldering and takes about 30min to assemble. **Electronics kit:** https://www.oskitone.com/product/space-dice-diy-electronics-kit **Assembly guide with printing and soldering instructions:** https://oskitone.github.io/space_dice/ **Source code:** https://github.com/oskitone/space_dice
