title: Space Dice description: Combination space laser noise and electronic dice machine tags: toy gaming dice noise openscad opensource oskitone These are the 3D-print files for the Oskitone Space Dice, a combination space laser noise and electronic dice machine. Press its button to get a random number between one and six and a near-faithful recreation of classic science fiction blaster firing sounds. Its accompanying DIY Electronics Kit requires through-hole soldering and takes about 30min to assemble. Electronics kit: https://www.oskitone.com/product/space-dice-diy-electronics-kit Assembly guide with printing and soldering instructions: https://oskitone.github.io/space_dice/ Source code: https://github.com/oskitone/space_dice
