Assemble enclosure

Work in Progress
These docs are still a work-in-progress and may not be fully baked just yet! Please contact me if any of it seems very wrong or needs extra clarification.

Steps​

  1. Cover battery
    • Optional: Use a piece of tape to affix the rectangular battery cover to the to the bottom edge of the battery, like so.
    • This is purely aesthetic! Skip if you'd like to show off the battery for some reason.
  2. Cover controls
    1. Set all the potentiometers to "noon" with their markers right in the middle (see picture above). Then slide on their knobs to the same alignment. Push them down all the way.
    2. Push the two top switch clutches onto SW1 and SW4.
    3. Insert the side switch clutch around SW3's actuator on the left of the PCB.
    4. Place light display atop D1 through D6.
      • Rotate it so the square with just one dot is in the top right, D1.
      • Again, it's okay if it's a little loose.
      • Fun fact: the little dots that dice have are called pips! pip pip pip pip
    5. Before moving on, test that all the newly covered controls are still usable and nothing is obstructed. Switch clutches still toggle switches, knobs turn pots, etc.
  3. Line up the enclosure top's holes to the controls, then snap the two enclosure halves together.
    • You may need to wiggle the light display around to fit into its cavity.
    • It's an intentionally tight fit; be brave!

Not working as expected? Check the Assembly troubleshooting section. Otherwise...

All done!​
