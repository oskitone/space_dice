## What you'll be making

Space Dice is a combination space laser noise and electronic dice machine.

This guide walks you through how to solder and assemble it, step by step. It will take 30 minutes or so, not counting 3D-printing time.

### How to use your "Space Dice"

Click the big button to roll and adjust controls for tone/speed/etc.

1. Slide side switch to toggle power
2. Click this big button to roll
3. Roll results display here, with pip counts from 1 to 6
4. TONE: frequency tone/pitch
5. DROP: how long roll takes to drop or decay
6. VOL: volume
7. OCT: tone octave
8. INCR: increment speed for pip display
