- 3D-printed plastic is susceptible to warping under hot temperatures. Keep it out of prolonged direct sunlight.
- When the device seems to reset randomly, behave erratically, or have trouble lighting its LEDs, that's a sign that its batteries don't have enough voltage. [Open up the enclosure](opening-the-enclosure.md) and replace them.
- Remove battery for storage to prevent damage from battery corrosion.
